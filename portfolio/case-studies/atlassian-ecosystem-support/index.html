Specialized Support in the Atlassian Ecosystem

Context​

I provide specialized technical support for Jira Service Management (JSM), Jira Software, Confluence, and third-party applications such as Xray and Scriptrunner, among others. I handle incidents and requests originating from the JSM portal with a technical, functional, and user-oriented approach.

My experience includes resolving over 8,500 tickets, ranking #1 on the team in volume of resolved cases, which reflects consistency, efficiency, and a deep understanding of the Atlassian ecosystem.

When a case requires more detail, I coordinate calls or direct sessions with the client, which facilitates understanding the context, reproducing the problem in real time, and offering more precise and faster solutions.

Technical and Specialized Knowledge​

My support role requires a complete understanding of the Atlassian ecosystem. Unlike a one-off implementation, here the responsibility lies in knowing every process, every integration, and every tool to diagnose and resolve real-world production issues.

Below, I detail my specific technical capabilities in this context:

  • Jira Software and Jira Core (Advanced Administration)

    • Management of complex schemas (workflows, screens, schemes, issue types) used in multiple internal processes.
    • Advanced configuration and management of boards, including complex filters, permissions, dynamic columns, and project dependencies.
    • In-depth knowledge of advanced JQL, combined with:
      • PQL (Projectrack Query Language) for queries between issues and projects.
      • AQL (Assets Query Language) for dynamic filtering of objects and relationships.
    • Advanced queries with tools like Xray (e.g., Test Plans → Tests → Executions).
    • Configuration of:
      • Complex Issue Security Schemes.
      • Internal and public Issue Collectors.
      • Administrative, analytical, and operational dashboards.
    • Administration of advanced automations, use of smart values, multiple branches, and conditional rules.
    • Integrations via webhooks, Slack, Teams or other internal services.

  • Jira Service Management

    • Complete administration of:
      • Portals
      • Forms
      • Request Types
      • Layouts
    • Asset Expert (formerly Insight):
      • Creation of complete schemas
      • Relationships between objects
      • Validations and filters using advanced AQL
      • Dynamic object-based dashboards
    • Notification diagnostics using Notification Helper for complex JSM flows
    • Management of SLAs, queues, and specific support automations

  • Confluence (Advanced Administration)

    • Management of permissions, restrictions, and spaces.
    • Creation and management of templates and custom templates.
    • Advanced use of:
      • Issue Filter
      • Gliffy diagrams
      • Page versions
      • Comala Workflows
      • Table Transformer (SQL in Confluence)
      • Table Filter, Charts & Spreadsheets
      • Troubleshooting & Support Tools
    • Calendar management, advanced macros, and structured copies of spaces/pages

  • Import Management (Issues, Tests, Asset Objects, and Structured Data)

    • Bulk Import of Issues (CSV)

      • Adjustment and correction of complex mappings, custom fields, references between issues, dependent values, validation rules, and special formats.

    • Import of Tests in Xray

      • Import of Tests, Test Sets, Test Executions, and Test Plans via CSV or compatible structures.
      • Correction and normalization of poorly constructed client files (encoding errors, separators, missing fields, invalid sequences).

    • Advanced Imports in Assets (JSM)

      • Data Mapping configuration for JSON, CSV, and external sources.
      • Transformation and adjustment of JSON files to comply with hierarchical structures, parent-child relationships, references between objects, and dependent attributes.
      • Creation of Object Type Mappings, update rules, duplicate handling, and cross-reference control.
      • Incremental and full-sync imports according to the required strategy.
      • Diagnosing import errors using asset logs, validation AQLs, and tests on development schemas.

    • Correction of files submitted by clients

      • Identification and repair of common errors:
      • Incorrect coding
      • Inconsistencies in foreign keys
      • Incorrect delimiters
      • Invalid dates
      • Broken JSON structures
      • Poorly defined relationships between objects

    • Guided client support to ensure clean, consistent, and maintainable imports

  • Diagnostics and Troubleshooting

    • Analysis of issue history, audit logs, and workflow traces.
    • Reading and analyzing logs from Jira, ScriptRunner, and Ambassadors.
    • Running and testing scripts in the Script Console (ScriptRunner).
    • Complete simulation of the process as executed by the user to reproduce complex errors.
    • Use of development environments to replicate problems without impacting production.
    • Execution of reindexing by project or globally when required.
    • In Confluence: log analysis by downloading zip files for specific dates.

  • Marketplace App Management

    • ScriptRunner

      • Conditions, Validators, and Post-Functions
      • Scheduled Jobs
      • Behaviors
      • Listeners
      • Fragments
      • Scripted Custom Fields
      • REST Endpoints
      • Bulk Import of Options
      • Switch Users to Replicate Real-World Scenarios

    • Projectrack

      • Advanced PQL
      • Field and Dashboard Configuration
      • Cross-Project Reporting

    • XRay

      • Complete Mastery of the Testing Lifecycle: Test → Test Set → Test Execution → Test Plan
      • Advanced Queries and Traceability Between Issues and Plans

    • Assets (JSM)

      • Schema creation
      • Complex relationships and dependencies
      • Advanced AQL
      • Dynamic validations
      • Integration with workflows and automation

    • Other apps used

      • Elements Connect
      • Component Versions
      • EazyBI
      • Comala
      • (among others)

  • Security, Users, and Directories

    • Advanced administration of global permissions, project roles, and groups.
    • Solid understanding of permission hierarchies and restrictions.
    • User management from Crowd, directory synchronization, and user creation.

  • Governance and Continuous Improvement

    • Collaboration with vendors (Adaptavist, Xray, etc.) to evaluate new features and improvements.
    • Technical criteria for determining the feasibility of customizations or complex changes.

Analytical Approach and Logic-Based Diagnosis​

In addition to my experience in Atlassian administration, I possess strong programming skills, which allow me to:

  • Analyze and understand complex scripts, conditions, validators, and automations.
  • Accurately identify the exact point where an error originates within a process, workflow, or script.
  • Anticipate the correct diagnostic path from the ticket description, significantly reducing resolution times.
  • Avoid rework and streamline investigations in cases involving multiple tools (Jira, JSM, Confluence, Assets, Xray, ScriptRunner, etc.).

This combination of administrative and technical skills expedites each stage of support, allowing me to maintain optimal response times and a resolution rate well above average.

This approach is one of the main reasons why I have resolved more than 8,500 tickets, ranking first in the number of closed cases within the team.

Results​

Specialized support has enabled:

  • Efficient and accurate incident resolution.
  • Reduction of unnecessary escalations through in-depth technical analysis.
  • Maintaining the stability of internal business processes.
  • Ensuring proper access and optimal performance of the Atlassian ecosystem.
  • Optimizing collaboration between teams and vendors.

Current Status​

The service is active and operational, handling multiple daily tickets related to Jira, JSM, Confluence, Crowd, and third-party tools.
